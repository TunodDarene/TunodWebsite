Dream Places

by: Darene Hanz H. Tunod

Russia – Moscow

I really want to visit Moscow because it always looks so cool in movies. Big open squares, colorful buildings, and crazy city vibes make it look like an action scene all the time. I’ve seen it in movies where spies run across rooftops and car chases happen, and it makes me want to walk around there for real. I’d start by exploring the city center and the famous streets where all the big movie scenes were filmed. The architecture looks wild, and I’d love to take pictures and just soak in the huge scale of everything. Even outside the main spots, there’s so much to see in Moscow, like smaller streets, markets, and places people actually live. Seeing it in person would feel so different from watching it in movies, and I want to feel that energy for myself.
