Dream Places

by: Darene Hanz H. Tunod

France – Paris and Beyond

France is one of those places I’ve always wanted to explore. Paris looks so alive in movies and shows, with the streets, cafes, and all the iconic spots. I’d love to just walk around, see the Eiffel Tower up close, and snap photos of all the cool architecture.

I really wanna try the food too croissants fresh from a bakery, fancy pastries, cheese, and real French bread. Sitting at a little cafe, tasting all the local flavors while watching the city go by, just like in the movies, would be amazing.

Beyond Paris, I’d love to see the countryside, old castles, and riverside towns. France seems like the perfect mix of history, food, and scenery, and I really wanna experience it all for real, not just on the screen.
