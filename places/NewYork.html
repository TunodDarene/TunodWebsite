Dream Places

by: Darene Hanz H. Tunod

USA – New York

I’ve played around Liberty City a lot in GTA, and I know it’s based on New York. Driving through the tall buildings, busy streets, and bright lights in the game made the city feel crazy and alive. Even though it’s not the real place, it gave me an idea of what New York might feel like.

That’s why I wanna see the real New York someday. I wanna stand in Times Square and see the giant screens in person, not just in a game. I wanna look up at the skyline and see how tall everything really is.

Liberty City is cool in GTA, but I know the real New York would feel way bigger and more real. It would be crazy to finally visit a place that I’ve only explored with a controller.
