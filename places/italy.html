Dream Places

by: Darene Hanz H. Tunod

Exploring Rome

I really wanna go to Rome someday. I’ve seen it in a lot of movies, shows, and even anime, and it always looks cool. The city just looks different from anywhere else. There are old buildings everywhere and it feels like you’re walking inside history.

Yeah I wanna see the Colosseum because it looks huge and crazy in videos, but I don’t just wanna see one place. I wanna walk around the whole city, check out random streets, see the fountains, and just explore. Rome looks like the kind of place where you can just walk around and still have fun.

I also wanna try real pizza and pasta there because I know it won’t taste the same anywhere else. Seeing Rome online is one thing, but actually being there would probably feel way different.
