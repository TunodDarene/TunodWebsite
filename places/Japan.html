Dream Places

by: Darene Hanz H. Tunod

Japan – Tokyo and Beyond

Japan has always been on my list. The streets of Tokyo look insane bright lights everywhere, packed trains, tiny ramen shops on every corner. I’ve seen scenes like this in so many anime and movies, and it makes me want to step inside that world for real.

I really wanna check out the spots where those crazy car racing scenes were filmed in Tokyo it’s like seeing Tokyo Drift in real life! Of course, I’ll explore the busy city, take in the neon signs, and then hit the quieter towns, temples, and gardens to see a different side of Japan. Walking around, trying street food, and feeling the city energy would be unreal.
