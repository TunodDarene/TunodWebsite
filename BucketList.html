My Bucket List

by: Darene Hanz H. Tunod

Stay happy

No matter what happens in life, I want to protect my peace and enjoy the journey. This is my number one goal and the most important thing to me.

Graduate from college
I want to finish my studies and make my family proud. Graduation is not just a ceremony, it’s proof that I worked hard and reached a milestone in my life.

Build a successful career in tech
I want to become successful in my work, solve problems, and create useful things. Being successful means I can achieve my dreams and inspire others to follow theirs.

Try food from multiple countries
I want to taste dishes from different cultures and enjoy the flavors of the world. This is exciting and teaches me about other people and their traditions.

Go skydiving
I want to jump from a plane and face my fears. Conquering this challenge will make me braver and remind me that I can overcome anything scary in life.

Create something fun and meaningful
I want to make a project, like an app or a creative work, that I enjoy making and that others can enjoy too. Sharing this makes life more exciting and rewarding.
